Soft Idli

Traditional South Indian steamed rice cakes

Prep Time: 8 hours (including soaking) | Cook Time: 10 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 1 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 4-6 hours
  2. Grind dal to smooth paste, rice to slightly coarse paste
  3. Mix together, add salt, and ferment overnight
  4. Grease idli plates, pour batter, steam for 10 minutes
  5. Serve hot with sambar and chutney

Authentic Sambar

Flavorful lentil stew with vegetables

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup toor dal
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (drumsticks, carrot, pumpkin)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp sambar powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves for tempering

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook dal until soft
  2. Cook vegetables in water with turmeric
  3. Add cooked dal, sambar powder, tamarind, and salt
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves in oil
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves

Crispy Dosa with Chutney

South Indian fermented crepe with coconut chutney

Prep Time: 8 hours | Cook Time: 5 mins per dosa

Ingredients (Dosa):

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients (Coconut Chutney):

  • 1 cup grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp roasted chana dal
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 inch ginger
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves for tempering

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 4-6 hours
  2. Grind to smooth batter and ferment overnight
  3. Heat griddle, spread batter thin, cook until crisp
  4. For chutney: Blend coconut, chana dal, chilies, ginger with water
  5. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves
  6. Serve hot dosa with chutney and sambar

Vanilla Sponge Cake

Light and fluffy homemade cake

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 30-35 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each
  4. Mix in vanilla
  5. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in separate bowl
  6. Alternately add flour mixture and milk to butter mixture
  7. Pour into greased cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes
  8. Cool before serving

Homemade Pizza

Classic wood-fired style pizza

Prep Time: 2 hours (dough) | Cook Time: 10-12 mins

Ingredients (Dough):

  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar

Ingredients (Topping):

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water, let sit for 10 mins
  2. Mix flour and salt, add yeast mixture and olive oil
  3. Knead for 10 mins until smooth and elastic
  4. Let dough rise for 1-2 hours until doubled
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C)
  6. Roll out dough, add sauce, cheese and toppings
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden

Creamy Alfredo Pasta

Rich and creamy Italian-style pasta

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 cups grated parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions
  2. Melt butter in large pan over medium heat
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant
  4. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes
  5. Gradually stir in parmesan until melted and smooth
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Toss with drained pasta and garnish with parsley

Chicken Biryani

Fragrant Indian rice dish with spiced chicken

Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice for 30 minutes, then parboil (70% cooked)
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt, biryani masala, ginger-garlic paste, salt for 1 hour
  3. In heavy pot, sauté onions until golden
  4. Add marinated chicken and cook for 10 minutes
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until soft
  6. Layer half the rice, then chicken mixture, then herbs, then remaining rice
  7. Sprinkle saffron milk, cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving

Mixed Berry Smoothie

Refreshing and nutritious fruit blend

Prep Time: 5 mins | Cook Time: 0 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk or almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add more milk if too thick, or ice if too thin
  4. Pour into glass and serve immediately
